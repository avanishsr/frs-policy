## What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon successful verification, the following data will be permanently deleted:
- User account information
- Profile details
- Login credentials
- App usage data associated with the account

## Data Retention

Certain non-personal data may be retained for legal, security, or compliance purposes for up to **30 days**, after which it is automatically removed.

## Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within **7 working days**.
